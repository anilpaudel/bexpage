

ORGANIZATION AND MANAGEMENT
ME 708
Lecture : 3 Year : IV
Tutorial : 1 Part : I
Practical : 0
Course Objectives:
 Acquire knowledge in the field of organizational management and
internal organization of companies required for managing an enterprise
 Acquire knowledge in the field of personnel management, motivation
and leadership for developing managerial skills
 Gain knowledge for starting a small scale unit independently
 Gain knowledge on case study and management information system.
